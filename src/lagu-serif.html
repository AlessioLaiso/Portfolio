Lagu Serif

Contemporary serif - 18 styles

About this font family

Lagu Serif blends a geometric inspiration with warm humanist elements, making it the perfect choice for when you need a fresh, contemporary serif typeface. Alessio Laiso has designed Lagu Serif in 18 styles: 9 weights ranging from Thin to Black, with matching, beautiful italics. The companion Lagu Sans makes the Lagu family a real workhorse for any use, including web, digital, print, branding and signage.

Lagu Serif has a large x-height and open counterforms, making it easily readable. It comes with powerful OpenType features, including ligatures, alternative glyphs, small caps, fractions, tabular figures, old-style figures, and more. The Lagu family supports 219 languages, covering 100% of the Latin Plus character set.
