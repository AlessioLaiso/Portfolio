Alessio Laiso is a designer at IBM in Dublin.

Alessio has designed award-winning software and experiences enjoyed by millions of people around the world. In addition to this, he has been designing typefaces since 2013. His fonts have been used by brands like eBay, Amazon, and USA Today.

Before joining IBM, Alessio has previously worked in Rome at Vertigo Design - for clients like Mercedes-Benz, P&G, and the European Space Agency - and in Austria at Runtastic (adidas Group), on mobile fitness apps.
