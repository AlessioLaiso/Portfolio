Alessio Laiso. Software and type design.

Alessio has designed award-winning software and experiences enjoyed by millions of people around the world. In addition to this, he has been designing typefaces since 2013. His fonts have been used by brands like eBay, Amazon, and USA Today.

Before joining Zalando in Lisbon, Alessio has previously worked in Dublin with IBM and CarTrawler, in Austria at Runtastic (adidas Group), and in Rome at Vertigo Design - for clients like Mercedes-Benz, P&G, and the European Space Agency.
